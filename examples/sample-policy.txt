Internal Security Policy (Sample Excerpt)

- Access control: Role-based access (RBAC) is required. Privileged access must use MFA.
- Data protection: Sensitive data must be encrypted at rest (AES-256 or equivalent) and in transit (TLS 1.2+).
- Patching: Critical CVEs must be remediated within 7 days; other patches on a regular cycle.
- Audit: Security-relevant actions must be logged and retained for at least 90 days.
